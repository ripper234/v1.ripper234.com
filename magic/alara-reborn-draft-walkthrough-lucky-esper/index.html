Alara Reborn Draft Walkthrough – Lucky Esper

Some drafts, you open a pack of trash and feel, from the very start, “this draft is not going my way”.

Others, you get lucky…

Pick 14

I'd like to say the great deck I drafted had anything to do with my skills, but in this case it's mostly luck. Getting a great 1st pick like Silence and discovering the two players feeding you want you to be in white is a great feeling. I went about 4-1 against a casual opponent, with the one game I lost I was at 3 lands and drawn half my deck with Mask of Riddles! Some games are just impossible. The decklist:

Creatures

  • Tower Gargoyle
  • Sanctum Gargoyle
  • Esper Battlemage
  • Blood Tyrant
  • Paragon of the Amesha
  • Master Transmuter
  • Aven Trailblazer
  • Darklit Gargoyle
  • Aerie Mystics
  • Ethersworn Shieldmage
  • 2 X Arsenal Thresher
  • Architects of Will
  • Glassdust Hulk
  • Wall of Denial
  • Esper Stormblade
  • Etherium Abomination

Spells

  • Resounding Silence
  • Agony Warp
  • 2 X Manaforce Mace
  • Mask of Riddles
  • Deny Reality

    • Lands

    • Seaside Citadel
    • Rupture Spire
    • 4 X Swamp
    • 5 X Island
    • 5 X Plains
    • Mountain
    Think I shouldn't have spliced red for the Tyrant? Don't understand why I didn't take Necromancer's Covenant? As always, your comments are appreciated.
