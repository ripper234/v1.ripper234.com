Rise of Eldrazi draft walkthrough

It’s been a while since I posted a walkthrough, mostly beacuse I’ve been busy with some projects. I’m enjoying Rise of Eldrazi draft immensly, but I always like a new set 🙂

Pick 1

Spoiler:
An obvious pick, and a lovely way to start the draft. Other cards to note are Domestication, Kozilek's Predator and Flame Slash.

Spoiler:
Nice, got two good white cards here so I'm assuming the player to my right is not into white. Knight of Cliffhaven is stronger because it's an offensive card. There are good green, red and black cards here, but I prefer to solidify my hold on white.
