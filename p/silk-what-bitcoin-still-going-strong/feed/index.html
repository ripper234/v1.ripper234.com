Yes, the price temporarily dropped, but the panics are becoming smaller and the recoveries are becoming faster, as bitcoin goes through this “price discovery” phase.

The price got ahead of its “true value” earlier this year, as people rushed into Bitcoins, bidding the price up, and paying a premium. Now, as bitcoin’s true value becomes greater, as more merchants accept it, as more tools become available, and as it becomes more inevitable that Bitcoin will be around for a while, the current premium over the “true value” becomes less and less. The drops on negative news are smaller and the bounce-back is swifter.
